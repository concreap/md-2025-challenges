## Rubrics for Evaluation

| Criteria                           | Description                                                | Score |
| :--------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------- | :---- |
| **Design System Thinking**         | Consistency, reusability, and scalability of components.   | 25%   |
| **States & Interaction Clarity**   | Coverage and clarity of component states and interactions. | 25%   |
| **Visual Consistency & Hierarchy** | Cohesion across layout, typography, and color usage.       | 20%   |
| **Documentation & Scalability**    | Clear system rules and principles for future growth.       | 15%   |
| **Flow Demonstration**             | Realistic and functional flows showcasing system usage.    | 15%   |

**Total:** 100%
